Who we are

We are a savings platform dedicated to helping our clients attain financial stability through saving and investing. Get started below to join our team and attain financial stability


App Features

Savings Challenges

These challenges are various ways to save money over a set period of time.
The 52 week challenge involves saving a set amount of money each week for a year.
The 26 week challenge involves saving the same amount over a six-month period.

Expense Tracker

The Expese tracker provides a sense of accomplishment and satisfaction, which can further motivate users to continue saving.
Tracking savings progress can help users identify areas where they may be overspending or where they can cut back in order to save more.

Budget Tracker

The Budget Tracker helps users stay on track with their spending and saving goals.
Through this tool, users can develop better financial habits, reduce their debt, and ultimately build stronger financial stability.

How it Works

To get started with Alterna savings, first create an account
Use your account details to login and access your dashboard, from where you will have access to all our app features
From there, you can subscribe to any savings challenge between the 52-week challenge and the 26-week challenge
Additionally, you can use our budget tracker to plan and monitor your monthly, or yearly budgets as you please, or the expense tracker to monitor your spending patterns over a monthly or annual period.
