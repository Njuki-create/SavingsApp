Our story

AlternaSavings was founded in 2023 with the vision to create a savings culture among the youth. The idea stemmed from the realization that there was a lack of effective platforms to encourage saving habits and investment options for young people. Our team recognized the importance of developing innovative tools that would make saving and investing more accessible and engaging for the younger generation.

With this in mind, we set out to create a solution that would not only make it easier for users to save but also offer exciting saving challenges and investment options. Our app provides a fun and social way for users to engage in saving, and joint challenges allow users to involve friends and family, creating a supportive community for achieving financial goals.

Our passion for promoting a culture of saving continues to drive us, and we are dedicated to empowering the next generation to achieve financial security and freedom.

Vision and Mission

Our vision at AlternaSavings is to become the go-to platform for the youth seeking to build a savings habit, improve their financial literacy, and invest in their future, all while having fun and connecting with their friends and family.

Our mission at AlternaSavings is to empower and encourage the youth to develop a strong savings culture and make smart investment decisions. We aim to achieve this by providing innovative and engaging saving challenges and investment options that are accessible to everyone, while fostering a sense of community and support.

Values

At AlternaSavings, we believe that saving should be a part of everyone's daily routine. We are passionate about promoting a saving culture among the youth and empowering them to achieve their financial goals.

Our values revolve around trust, simplicity, and inclusivity. We strive to build a platform that is user-friendly, transparent, and accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial background. We prioritize the security and privacy of our users' data and ensure that their information is protected at all times.

We are committed to providing our users with a variety of saving challenges and investment options that cater to their unique needs and preferences. We believe that by working together, we can create a better financial future for ourselves and our loved ones.

Join us at AlternaSavings and start your journey towards financial freedom today!
